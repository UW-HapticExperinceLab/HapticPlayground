About the Haptic Playground Workshop

The Haptic Playground Workshop is a visionary convening for the CHI 2024 symposium, designed to democratize haptic design and empower creators from all backgrounds to innovate within this transformative space. Our mission is to dismantle the technical and interdisciplinary barriers that have traditionally segmented haptic research and practice.

This one-day workshop offers an unparalleled opportunity for both established experts and new hapticians to collaborate and break through disciplinary silos. Featuring keynote talks from esteemed figures in the field and interactive sessions with innovative tangible tools, we aim to foster an environment where diverse perspectives unite to forge a more inclusive and universally accessible haptic future.

Join us as we explore tangible toolkits like the Tickle Trunk and VibroHarness System, which provide immediate and intuitive means for designing haptic sensations. These sessions are crafted to not only inspire but also to articulate and evaluate design intentions, adhering to inclusive design principles that respect and recognize the unique needs of each individual.

We are on a quest to craft a comprehensive haptic design checklist that embodies the diverse requirements of our community, ensuring that every touch, every interaction, is both meaningful and accessible. Be part of this groundbreaking event and contribute to shaping an inclusive trajectory for haptic technology.

For more details on our objectives, structure, and how to participate, please visit our workshop about, schedule, and call for participation pages.
